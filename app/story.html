Каков состав панелей?

For most people, installing the panels would be an entertaining DIY project. It is very simple - you can cut the panels with almost any saw and fix it with screws through the felt. It could not be simpler.

If you prefer to watch DIY projects only on TV, then you should hire a joiner/carpenter to do the work for you. It shouldn't take long - your local joiner could easily install it for you. Just show the installation instructions.
